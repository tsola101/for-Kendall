Someone asked me recently, why do you love her and don’t say cuz she’s hot or nice or caring , look deeper.

the person you love is also the person who triggers you the most, and it’s not a coincidence it’s how love actually works, as my partner you’re not meant to just make me happy, youre there to show me parts of myself I didn’t know existed, the parts I’ve been avoiding, the wounds that haven’t healed, the patterns i keep repeating. The way you challenge me, the way you make me feel exposed and vulnerable, that’s not a problem with our relationship, it’s love doing its job. Real love doesn’t just comfort you, it forces you to change and grow, it holds up a mirror to your habits and deepest fears. It makes you confront the parts of yourself you ignore. That’s why relationships r so hard, you’re not just dealing with another person but the parts of you they bring out in you that you thought you had handled. Love isn’t meant to be easy it’s meant to change you. And I know I love you because it’s what makes me not want to stop ever. It’s what inspires me to be better for us. Even when people roll their eyes or call me crazy or you push me away even then especially then, love shows up in moments when it’s not expected to. Love isn’t just a feeling, it’s what you do in spite of what you feel, you just don’t give up, Real love doesn’t walk away it knocks until you’re brave enough to open the door because if I could give up and listen to everyone then it wasn’t really love and deep down I know what I feel for you. I know that I don’t just love you, I’m devoted to you. When I really think about it, I fell for you twice. I didn’t just fall for the loving caring beautiful parts of you, I also fell for the part of you that you hide from most people, the part of you that’s both good and bad. But irregardless of all that I still choose you.

I care about you deeply and I love you deeply, I don’t just love who you are now I love you for who I know you’re gonna become too. And I wannabe here for that. And I believe that I am willing to give to this relationship on a level that is irreplaceable. And I know that there are things you wish were different about this relationship or you wouldn’t hv ended it. I am prepared to do everything in my power to work on things that I need to work on and if there’s genuinely anything that falls into my lap that I need to work on, I am willing to do that work because this relationship matters to me. But it takes 2, if you want this work and you care about me and love me on the level that I do you I trust that you’ll give it a shot but if you genuinely don’t want to give me the chance to fight for this then you must be right and this isn’t what I thought it was. So I can’t make u say yes to trying this again but I can tell you what I’m willing to do and what I would love to do to make this work. I’ll end it with this tho, Grass isn’t only Green on the other side, grass is greener wherever you water it.

— Adrian
