For someone so amazing, talented, and sharp, I still can’t believe there’s one thing you just can’t do. It’s such a simple task, yet somehow, it’s your kryptonite. The word is “treadmill,” not “tread-meal.” Every time you say it, I can’t help but laugh. You say it so sincerely that correcting you almost feels wrong. It’s adorable seeing you genuinely puzzled, trying to figure out how it’s any different. So consider this page your little training ground , your 1 moment of redemption. Say it with me: “TRED-mill.” Not “Tred-Meal.”
